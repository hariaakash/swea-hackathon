About

Code Enigma is a coding and product development based competition being organized by the Software Engineering Association, SRM University, Chennai. It's a 3 round competition in which the first two rounds are purely coding based and the last round is a full blown 20 hours product development. This event gives students an opportunity to experience two of the most popular code based competitive formats that are prevalent today. We are sure this outlook will be appreciated and will generate participation not only among seasoned coders but also among amateurs and those who are new to coding.

FAQ

How do I register ?

To register for this event, click this button https://events.sweassociation.in/#!/register

What about food and drinks ?

Food and drinks are on us, but you need to bring your own linen.

What is the venue for this challenge?

The venue for 2nd round is: 7th Floor SRM Labs, University Building (Library Building), SRM University, Kattankulathur. Venue for 3rd round: Mini Hall 2, T.P. Ganesan Auditorium, SRM Nagar, SRM Univeristy, Kattankulathur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 603203

Who will own the IP(Intellectual Property) Rights to the product that I have built?

The developer/developers of the web/mobile application will have all rights and own the IP of the product. However, all code needs to be in public domain (open source) so that it can be evaluated by the judges.

Do I need to bring my laptop?

Yes. And a charger as well.

Is it an individual or team challenge?

It's an team challenge consists of maximum 4 members but individual participants are also encouraged to participate and form a single member team.

How to reach SRM?

Link to google maps: https://goo.gl/maps/sC1Da72WXE42 From Airport : Reach the nearest local train station(Tirusulam) and get on a train towards Chengalpattu and get down at Potheri station. Nearest Railway Station : Potheri. Landmark : ZOHO office, Estancia township. You can always opt for a cab to SRM University Kattankulathur.

For queries and updates ?

Do checkout our Facebook page for updates and for queries you can message us on Facebook, use the Ask Us feature from the bottom of the page or mail us at support@sweassociation.in
